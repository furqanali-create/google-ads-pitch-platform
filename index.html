About the Pitch Platform

This tool is designed to empower Google Ads Account Managers at MarketStar with a structured, effective, and consistent framework for client conversations.

The goal is to move beyond simple recommendations and guide clients through a logical journey of optimization, starting with foundational elements like conversion tracking, progressing through the strategic selection of a bidding strategy, and addressing crucial topics like budget and potential objections.

By following the built-in call flow and leveraging the tailored scripts, you can deliver high-impact consultations that drive client success and build stronger partnerships.
